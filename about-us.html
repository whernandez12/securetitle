Latin America’s Title & Escrow Experts

Since 1997, our services have been designed to help you navigate foreign real estate transactions, protecting you from unnecessary risks and overwhelming processes. With years of experience and tens of thousands of transactions successfully managed, Secure Title has adapted the familiar US real estate closing model to international real estate transactions. Our title guaranty/insurance policies, escrow and closing services are crafted to address the specific complexities and legal requirements of the countries we serve. Secure Title operates through a network of offices in Mexico, Central America and the Caribbean, with escrow infrastructure in the United States. Each regional office provides a complete range of services that provide transparency, security and peace of mind to your transactions. When investing in real estate, you can trust Secure Title’s financial strength, professional service and leading technology to execute your transaction flawlessly.
