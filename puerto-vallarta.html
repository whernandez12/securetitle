Services

Escrow Title Guaranty & Insurance Policies Closing Services Title Report & Title Search

When purchasing property, one of the most important considerations for both a buyer and a seller is whether and under what conditions the transaction funds will be safely held and distributed by an independent party.

In an international transaction, this issue is even more vital and often complicated, as either the purchaser or the seller may not be familiar or comfortable having funds deposited in an unfamiliar jurisdiction or with an unfamiliar attorney or bank. Utilizing Secure Title’s escrow services provides cost-effective security to all parties in the real estate transaction.

Secure Title escrow team administers deposited funds ensuring an efficient and secure disbursement process.

With over $5 billion in coverage issued, Secure Title issues more title guaranty and title insurance policies than any other provider in the region. Trust us to protect your ownership and guard against hidden risks. For a fee typically around 0.5%, put chance to rest.

Beyond providing customers title guaranty and escrow services, Secure Title provides a host of coordination and closing services to help streamline the real estate transaction process. Acting as a single point of contact for closing details, Secure Title takes the worry from your hands and we lead you to a successful close. When utilizing Secure Title closing services, your transaction is managed by a highly trained closer, who will guide you through the details and ensure an efficient process.

We also provide service to many major US and international law firms, several of the top tier international banks and many Fortune 500 companies, as well as the US government.
